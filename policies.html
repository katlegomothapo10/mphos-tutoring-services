Guidelines

Our Policies

Clear, fair policies to ensure a smooth and professional tutoring experience.

💰

Deposit Requirement

A R50 non-refundable deposit is required for all standalone sessions to secure your booking. This deposit is deducted from the session fee.

⏰

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations require 24 hours notice. Late cancellations may forfeit the deposit or session fee. This allows us to offer the time slot to other learners.

🔄

Tutor Cancellations

If the tutor needs to cancel for any reason, the session will be rescheduled at no extra cost to you. Your learning progress remains our priority.

📶

Online Sessions

Online learners require stable internet and a suitable device for video conferencing. We recommend a quiet space to maximise focus and learning.

Have questions about our policies? Contact us – we're happy to help.
